Services

SLG Project Management provides flexible services that allow you to get the best out of your projects. We will listen, be open and honest and work hard to achieve your goals.

Project Managment

Project Management is a process by which projects are defined, planned , monitored, controlled and delivered such that the benefits are realised.

When working alongside our customers we at SLG would follow the gate/stage process, where no other Project Management process existed.

Project Management Office

We will create or support your existing PMO as a way to boost IT efficiencies, standardise processes, cut costs to improve on Project deliveries. This will include:

  • Administrative support and assistance to project managers
  • Collection, analysis and reporting of project information
  • Assurance of Project Management Processes
  • Provision of additional tools, techniques and information
