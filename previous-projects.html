Previous Projects

We have 24 years experience of Project Management. Here are some examples of the nature of the projects we cover.

Security Projects

We have implemented projects and key governance mechanisms to help shape control and track change across the Security spectrum. This would include projects to:

  • Implement Information Assurance Accreditation
  • Implement Security infrastructure projects following ISO27001 standards
  • Track Business as usual incidents/ queries / scan results to closure

In addition we would provide weekly/monthly reports to management that clearly identify achievement , issues & risks, planned versus actual budget and next activities with a helicopter view of Key Performance Indicators relating to security areas across the company.

Engineering Projects

We see the benefits from Engineering, Security and IT working closely together and have structured projects to capitalise on the skills that each function can bring. Our projects have included:

  • Centralising Engineering standard secure applications to speed up project startup times, complete with full documentation and test and training packages.
  • Setting up governance mechanisms to ensure Engineering / Security / IT, buyin up front leading to further efficiencies.
  • Preparing a Service IT catalogue for Engineers to make it quick and easy to select what they need, when they need it.

Infrastructure Projects

Delivering multi-site projects can be hard without proper programme support. We have experience in delivering project governance and monitoring mechanisms to clearly track what can be an ever changing situation. Recent projects have been:

  • Implementation of Active Directory, Email Centralisation and Livemeeting delivering 8500 upgrades to PCs across 52 sites
  • Relocation of secure infrastructure from London to the midlands over a 3 month period, devising repeatable processes to plan, move, test equipment and review learn lessons for next moves, taking health and safety into consideration at all times.

Assets Projects

Taking control of your Asset estate is key to having a secure environment. Therefore we also have experience in:

  • Working with suppliers to define and execute full scale audits
  • Implementing a new Asset Management application system
  • Working with Site IT Leads to implement new asset management processes for continued accuracy
  • Developing measures to monitor and track on a continual basis.
